Physical Proximity

Movement triggers notes. As devices (phones, watches) enter the room or move closer to your machine, the chords grow richer, louder, and spread across octaves.

Generative Ensembles

Forget standard MIDI playback. bleTones renders native polyphonic synthesis across 6 curated ensembles (Choral, Airy, Shimmer, Bells) with zero-latency buffer management.

The Helper Architecture

Bypass restrictive DAW sandboxes. A lightweight, standalone background app sniffs raw CoreBluetooth/WinRT data and blasts it over local OSC to the VST.
